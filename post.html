Waterboy

An unassuming fool becomes a football star.

Posted by Anthony Guillen on November 26, 2022

Waterboy follows the story of Bobby Boucher, a man who has been a "water distribution manager" his entire life, gets fired, and randomly has the chance to become a college football star.

Work Hard, Play Harder

Bobby ends up playing well but the team they have to play in the championship is the old college team that fired him. They let it out that he does not have a high school degree and can not play college football any longer. The NCAA is convinced to allow Bobby to get his GED so he could continue playing. Bobby works hard and gets that GED and shows up at the halftime of the championship game to his team losing badly.

Remember the time Bobby Boucher showed up at halftime and the Mud Dogs won the Bourbon Bowl, do ya?!

The team ends up winning the championship through teamwork.

Other believed in him so he started to believe in himself.
