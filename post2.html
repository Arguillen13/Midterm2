Billy Madison

School is not for fools.

Posted by Anthony Guillen on September 29, 2022

Billy Madison is the story of a rich man whose father paid his teachers off throughout high school. Billy finds out he does not have an actual eduction when his father wants to pass down his company to his son.

The Importance of Eduction

While going through school again Billy learns a lot of important lessons throughout all of his grades. He learns how to be compassionate towards others and learns what is really important to him. He ultimately graduates high school and actually gives the company to a family friend who wants to keep the business thriving.

Billy takes one for the team.
